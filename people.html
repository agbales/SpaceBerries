Our Organizers

Nancy Organ
Founder and Seattle Organizer

Nancy started DVJS in June 2016 to create a space for the nascent data vis commuity to come together. She studied Statistics and Biology at UC Berkeley and started her career in Medical research.
Jessica Renaud
Ambassador in Chief, Montreal

Torn between her love of numbers and the visual arts for (almost) ever, Jessica has been able to reconcile her two passions with data visualization. For the past two years, she has been working to promote the subject in Montreal, as Ambassador in Chief of the Data Viz Jam Session, and through her involvement in various conferences and workshops. Jessica has a master’s degree in economics and currently works as a BI Analyst. See her work here.

Déchirée entre son amour pour les chiffres et les arts visuels depuis (presque)toujours, Jessica a pu réconcilier ses deux passions grâce à la visualisation de données. Depuis deux ans, elle travaille à faire connaître le sujet à Montréal, en tant qu’Ambassadrice en Chef des Data Viz Jam Session, ainsi que et par son implication dans diverses conférences et ateliers. Jessica est titulaire d'une maîtrise en économie et travaille actuellement en tant qu'analyste BI.
